Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random numer at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your Total Score.

You win the game by adding up the crystals so that your Total Score matches that of the number you were provided at the start of the game.

The value of each crystal will be hidden from you until it is clicked.

Each time the game starts over the values of each crystal will also change along with the random number.
